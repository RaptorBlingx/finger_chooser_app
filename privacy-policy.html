Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Finger Chooser ("the App") is a party game developed by RaptorBlingx (Mohamad Jarad). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects limited data to improve your experience:

  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as screen views, game modes played, and general app interaction patterns. This data does not personally identify you.
  • Crash Reports: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash logs and diagnostic data to improve app stability. This may include device model, OS version, and stack traces.
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your preferences.
  • Local Storage: The App stores your preferences (language, sound, vibration settings), game statistics, and unlocked dare packs locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the user experience
  • To diagnose and fix crashes and bugs
  • To display advertisements

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Analytics: Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Analytics and crash data is shared only with Firebase/Google for the purposes described above.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

6. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained by Firebase according to their standard retention policies. Local data stored on your device persists until you clear the App's data or uninstall the App.

7. Your Rights

  • You can reset your local data by clearing the App's storage in your device settings.
  • You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's ad settings.
  • You can request deletion of your analytics data by contacting us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
